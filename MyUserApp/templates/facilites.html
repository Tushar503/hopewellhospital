Facilities

We at Thunga Hospital offer our patients efficient medical services for a better healthcare treatment. Our medical services include


  • Ambulance Service

    Offer a prompt, 24X7 ambulance for emergency transport of patient.

  • Casualty & Emergency Unit

    A 24X7 operating casualty and emergency Unit for accident cases.

  • Pharmacy

    In-house 24X7 pharmacy offering medicines and other relevant medical treatment equipments.

  • Pathology & Diagnostic Center

    An around the clock operating pathology & diagnostic center for diagnosis of ailments.

  • Digital X-Ray / Portable X-Ray

    X-Ray facility for a simplified and hassle free experience for basic bone diagnosis.

  • Sonography / 2D Echo

    Hospitals are equipped with modern ultrasound machines that provide high resolution image and helps diagnose anatomical defects.

  • X-Ray

    Hospitals consist of X-ray machines featuring high frequency low radiation digital radiography.

  • Haemo Dialysis

    Hospital has a Haemo dialysis unit for providing kidney dialysis to ailing patients.

  • Ultrasound

    Hospitals also have Ultrasound machines offering 3D and 4D imaging and provides whole body Doppler.

Combined, best in class technology and expertise of professional doctors the hospital ensures world-class health care to all patients. Established in Mira road with a capacity of 120 beds, Malad with 150 beds and Boisar with 70 beds, all the hospitals are well equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and advanced information management system. Hospital's advanced building Management System offers a multi-tiered access control, electronic security systems with integrated CCTVs spanning across the facility and advanced fire management system amongst other utilities.


  • Laboratories

    Hospitals feature well equipped labs to offer range of diagnostic services in Haematology, Microbiology, Molecular Biology and Histopathology.

  • Blood storage centre

    Hospitals feature 24X7 blood storage center with all the required facilities in it to meet the blood requirements of patients in the hospital. The center is equipped with the NAT System for the screening of blood to ensure safety for all its patients.
